ELIXIR-UK is the UK node of [ELIXIR](https://elixir-europe.org), the European research infrastructure for life science data. We are a **network** of UK research-performing organisations: we advocate, coordinate, train, and set standards. Members deliver the work; the Node connects them.

## Mission

**Proposed 2025 (under review):**

> Supporting UK life science community to build, connect, and sustain world-class digital research infrastructure — promoting standards, shaping policy and amplifying UK voices.

_Previous (2019):_ "Helping UK life science researchers use biological information effectively, by promoting development of the required infrastructure."

The 2025 update reflects the 2024 SIAB review: shift from pure "infrastructure provision" toward **people, collaboration, and community-building**. Aligned with the UK 2021 Life Sciences Vision.

## Vision

**Proposed 2025 (under review):**

> A UK research ecosystem where every life-science researcher and technical professional shapes and benefits from a world-leading, collaborative and FAIR digital research infrastructure that is AI-ready.

_Previous: not articulated as a single sentence._

## Tagline

**Proposed 2025 (under review):**

> Empowering people. Supporting services. Advancing AI-readiness. Sustaining digital research infrastructure.

_Previous: none. ELIXIR parent has used "Data for life"._

## Values

From the previous comms plan; carry forward unless the next strategic review changes them.

- Financial sustainability
- Scientific rigour
- Collaboration
- Inclusiveness
- Respect

Not in priority order. How the brand behaves under load — when one is compromised, the others get heavier weight.

## Dual identity

For funders (ESFRI, EU, UKRI) we present as a **Research Infrastructure**. In day-to-day work we're a **distributed network of member organisations**. Both true. Lead with the framing that's most useful to the audience — see [Style guide](/style-guide).

## Cause, impact, actions

A sense-check framework when proposing new activities.

**Cause.** UK life-science researchers, in how they work with life-science data and information.

**Impact.** They carry out work more effectively and efficiently — greater insight, faster, FAIR practice, reproducible, more reliable tools. Reduces cost and time to create UK economic value from life-science data.

**Actions.** Strengthen interactions between people delivering UK life-science infrastructure. Raise awareness of ELIXIR-UK. Grow the consortium. Channel funding to members. Build diversity and inclusion. Engage UKRI, ELIXIR, RDA, and other external stakeholders.
