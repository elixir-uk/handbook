## File formats

- **SVG** is the default. Vector, scales cleanly at any size, smaller file than PNG, accessible to screen readers when alt text is provided. Use SVG everywhere it's supported.
- **PNG** is the fallback at 1200 × 1200 (logomark) or 1200 × 960 (logotype). Use when SVG isn't supported – older Word/PowerPoint, certain email clients, image-only contexts.
