Concrete examples — the fastest way to understand the voice. Each pair shows the same idea written off-brand and on-brand.

These examples are also what makes the [LLM system prompt](/llm-system-prompt) work: language models learn voice from examples better than from rules.

## Opening a webstory

| ✗ Don't | ✓ Do |
|---|---|
| _"We are excited to announce that ELIXIR-UK has launched a new training programme aimed at empowering the next generation of bioinformatics researchers."_ | _To author. Show the on-brand version._ |

**Why:** _to author. What was wrong with the don't, and what does the do fix?_
