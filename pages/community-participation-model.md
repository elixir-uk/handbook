ELIXIR-UK exists because a community of researchers, trainers, data stewards, and partners builds it. This section names that relationship: rules, roles, pathways, recognition.

The [Participation guidelines](/cp-guidelines) are the floor — start there. Everything else builds on top.

## Principles that shape the model

1. **Design for contribution, not consumption.** Every touchpoint should make it easier to get involved.
2. **Names go on things.** Contribution is named, not anonymised. Authorship matters.
3. **Meet people where they are.** Not every researcher wants to contribute — they're an audience, not the community.
4. **Share infrastructure, not just outputs.** The community owns the resources it builds.
